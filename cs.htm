COMPUTER SCIENCE

The Department of Computer Science offers 3-year B.Sc.(Hons.) Computer Science programme through constituent colleges of the University of Delhi.With the new advancements in the field of computers and in a time when there is a boom in the IT industry, the University of Delhi has introduced B.Sc (Hons.) Computer Science, a 3-year undergraduate programme, for the tech-savvy youth.The programme provides rigorous foundations of the concepts of Computer Science and Information Technology. In the final year, students also get an opportunity to do project work.
